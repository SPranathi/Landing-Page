Increadible India!

The geographic terrain of India is rich and diverse, filled with lush green forests,scenic lakes,majestic mountain peaks,ropical beaches,and vast stretches of wilderness. From east to west, north to south, this South Asian country packs an assortment of treasures for nature lovers.

Popular in India

Nubra Valley is located between Tibet and Kashmir and is known for its orchards, scenic vistas, Bactrian camels, and monasteries.

Coorg is a tiny hill station in Southern Karnataka. With its lush coffee plantations, stunning landscapes, quaint villages and cascading waterfalls, this place is irresistible.

Munnar is the ultimate place to be in the state with the merging of 3 mountain ranges. The tea plantation gives a vivid picturesque view of the town.

Loktak Lake of Manipur state is the largest freshwater lake in India and mostly famous for the floating phumdis. This ancient lake also host the only floating national park in the world.
